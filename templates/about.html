03 : About me

Hello, I'm Seth Grinstead, a passionate Game Programmer with a deep love for creating immersive experiences. Currently, I work as a manager at VRAdventures.Zone, where I'm not only leading the operations of the VR arcade but also developing the company’s very first in-house game. This opportunity has been a significant milestone in my journey, and I’m excited to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the world of VR and gaming.

I have a relentless dedication to learning and self-improvement. I’m not satisfied with "good enough" — I'm always striving for excellence. Every project, big or small, is an opportunity for me to grow, and I actively seek ways to refine my skills, whether through new technical challenges or by analyzing games from a creator's perspective.

While still early in my career, I've had the privilege of working on several projects that have helped shape my understanding of game development. A highlight of my experience was my Capstone project at Algonquin College, where I worked with a talented team of 10 students to design and develop a game from concept to release. Though the project had its challenges, it provided invaluable insights into both the craft of game development and the dynamics of teamwork.

Outside of work, I like to be adventurous. I love trying new things—whether it’s exploring new places, tasting different foods, or learning new skills. Recently, I’ve been diving into Python, which led me to create this website. My future goals include transitioning into Python development, with a focus on web development and machine learning, before eventually pivoting into offensive cybersecurity.
